Overview

The province of Vercelli is the 99th largest Italian province by population with 170.911 people, located in the Piedmont region.

The larger Vercelli metropolitan area comprises 82 towns (comuni) and covers an area of 208.164 km2. The population density is 82 inhabitants per km2, making it sparcely populated. The male to female ratio is 1.00:1.08.

Climate

The province of Vercelli receives on average 225 hours of sunshine per month, or 7.5 hours of sunshine per day. This is 4.66% less than the average for Italy and 1.35% more than the average for the region of Piedmont.

Throughout the year, it rains on average 7.02 days per month, which is an ordinary amount of precipitation for an Italian province.

Throughout the autumn and winter season, there are usually 14.79 days per month with fog and 5.54 cold days per month with perceived temperatures below 3°C. In the summer, there are on average 18.55 hot days per month with perceived temperatures above 30°C.

Cost of Living

The average monthly income in Vercelli is around 1917.62€, which is higher than the average for Italy.

The estimated cost of living is around 1691.34€ per month for an individual or 2560.87€ per month for a family of 4. The cost for renting a small apartment (2-3 bedrooms) in a main city area is around 767.9€ per month.

Overall, Vercelli is expensive compared to other Italian provinces. Living in Vercelli is around 2.50% more expensive than the average of all Italian provinces.

Quality of Life

Healthcare

Healthcare in Vercelli is above average. For every 10k inhabitants, there are around undefined pharmacies, 8.73 general practitioners and 24.9 specialized doctors per 10k inhabitants. Average life expectancy in Vercelli is 80.1 years of age.

Education

Vercelli has a higher-than-average percentage of high school graduates, around 63.9%; and a lower-than-average percentage of university graduates, around 26.3%. The average number of completed years of schooling for people over 25 is 10.26, which is not far from the national average of 10.44. There is one university in the province.

Leisure



Overall, Vercelli has pretty good nightlife with 3.46 bars and 3.48 restaurants per 10k inhabitants.

Crime and Safety

The province of Vercelli is overall very safe for expats. As of 2021, there are an average of 2354.1 reported crimes per 100k inhabitants. This is 16.99% lower than the national average.

There have been around 0.6 deadly road accidents and 8.4 serious work-related injuries per 10k people in Vercelli. This is respectively 11.11% more driving accidents than average and 34.88% less work accidents than average.

Car theft is reportedly 80.85% lower than average with only 13.51 cases per 100k inhabitants. Reports of house thefts are 42.29% lower than average with 101 cases per 100k inhabitants. Cases of robbery are uncommon with 101 reported cases per 100k inhabitants, about 65.54% less the national average.

Overall, Vercelli is a very safe place to visit and live in.

Transport

Public transport in Vercelli is lacking, and traffic is somewhat high. There are on average 0.67 active vehicles per person, against a national average of 0.66.

Around 1.97km per 10k inhabitants of the main city in Vercelli consist of bicycle lanes. This makes Vercelli somewhat bike-friendly by Italian standards.
