Overview

The province of Barletta-Andria-Trani is the 52nd largest Italian province by population with 390.011 people, located in the Apulia region.

The larger Barletta-Andria-Trani metropolitan area comprises 10 towns (comuni) and covers an area of 154.295 km2. The population density is 253 inhabitants per km2, making it somewhat densely populated. The male to female ratio is 1.00:1.03.

Barletta-Andria-Trani is a new province in Apulia in Italy to be split off from the provinces of Bari and Foggia in 2009.
