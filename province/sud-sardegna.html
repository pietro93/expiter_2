Climate

The province of Sud Sardegna receives on average 246 hours of sunshine per month, or 8.2 hours of sunshine per day. This is 4.24% more than the average for Italy and 0.49% more than the average for the region of Sardinia.

Throughout the year, it rains on average 6.46 days per month, which is below average for an Italian province.

Throughout the autumn and winter season, there are usually 3.65 days per month with fog and 0 cold days per month with perceived temperatures below 3°C. In the summer, there are on average 31.06 hot days per month with perceived temperatures above 30°C.
