Climate

The province of Frosinone receives on average 246 hours of sunshine per month, or 8.2 hours of sunshine per day. This is 4.24% more than the average for Italy and 1.44% less than the average for the region of Lazio.

Throughout the year, it rains on average 8.27 days per month, which is well above average for an Italian province.

Quality of Life

Healthcare

Healthcare in Frosinone is below average. For every 10k inhabitants, there are around undefined pharmacies, 8.91 general practitioners and 19.7 specialized doctors per 10k inhabitants. Average life expectancy in Frosinone is 81.9 years of age.

Education

Frosinone has a higher-than-average percentage of high school graduates, around 67.9%; and a higher-than-average percentage of university graduates, around 29.9%. The average number of completed years of schooling for people over 25 is 10.68, which is not far from the national average of 10.44. There are no universities in this province.

Leisure

Frosinone offers a lively and energetic nightlife scene that reflects the city's southern Italian spirit. The city features a range of bars, pubs, and clubs where locals and visitors can enjoy live music, DJ sets, and a bustling atmosphere. Frosinone's nightlife scene is known for its warm hospitality, friendly locals, and a variety of venues that cater to different tastes and preferences.

Overall, Frosinone has somewhat decent nightlife with 3.18 bars and 3.75 restaurants per 10k inhabitants.

Crime and Safety

The province of Frosinone is overall very safe for expats. As of 2021, there are an average of 2189 reported crimes per 100k inhabitants. This is 22.81% lower than the national average.

There have been around 0.4 deadly road accidents and 8.7 serious work-related injuries per 10k people in Frosinone. This is respectively 25.93% less driving accidents than average and 32.56% less work accidents than average.

Car theft is reportedly 67.56% lower than average with only 22.88 cases per 100k inhabitants. Reports of house thefts are 9.92% lower than average with 157.65 cases per 100k inhabitants. Cases of robbery are uncommon with 157.65 reported cases per 100k inhabitants, about 67.43% less the national average.

Overall, Frosinone is a very safe place to visit and live in.

Transport

Public transport in Frosinone is lacking, and traffic is very high. There are on average 0.8 active vehicles per person, against a national average of 0.66.

Around 1.35km per 10k inhabitants of the main city in Frosinone consist of bicycle lanes. This makes Frosinone somewhat bike-friendly by Italian standards.
