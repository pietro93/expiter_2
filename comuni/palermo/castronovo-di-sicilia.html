Castronovo di Sicilia is a municipality of 2.913 inhabitants located in the Palermo province in the Italian region of Sicily in Southern Italy. It has a population density of 14 people per km² and an altitude of 660 metres above the sea level.

Castronovo di Sicilia accounts for about 0.23% of the total population in the province of Palermo and about 0.00483% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Castronovo di Sicilia is classified as a Climate Zone D, which means it is a temperate town by Italian standards. The local climate is characterized by short, mild winters and long, hot and dry summers. Rainfall is mostly concentrated to the winter period.

The province of Palermo experiences on average 97.51 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 0.00 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 83.40 days per year. There is little to no fog throughout the year. Castronovo di Sicilia receives around 8.6 hours of sunshine per day on average.
