Pontecchio Polesine is a municipality of 2.195 inhabitants located in the Rovigo province in the Italian region of Veneto in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 190 people per km² and an altitude of 5 metres above the sea level.

Pontecchio Polesine accounts for about 0.93% of the total population in the province of Rovigo and about 0.00364% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.
