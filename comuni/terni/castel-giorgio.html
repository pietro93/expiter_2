Castel Giorgio is a municipality of 2.076 inhabitants located in the Terni province in the Italian region of Umbria in Central Italy. It has a population density of 49 people per km² and an altitude of 559 metres above the sea level.

Castel Giorgio accounts for about 0.92% of the total population in the province of Terni and about 0.00345% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Castel Giorgio is classified as a Climate Zone E, which means it is a fairly chill town. The local climate is characterized by long and cold winters with frequent snowfall in mountaineous areas, and somewhat warm and dry summers.

The province of Terni experiences on average 100.22 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 2.30 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 93.12 days per year. There are 21.19 foggy days throughout the year. Castel Giorgio receives around 8 hours of sunshine per day on average.
