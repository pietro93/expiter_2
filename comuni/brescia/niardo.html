Niardo is a municipality of 1.987 inhabitants located in the Brescia province in the Italian region of Lombardy in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 90 people per km² and an altitude of 442 metres above the sea level.

Niardo accounts for about 0.16% of the total population in the province of Brescia and about 0.00330% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Niardo is classified as a Climate Zone E, which means it is a fairly chill town. The local climate is characterized by winters which are long and cold and summers which are dry and very hot. There are frequent precipitations in autumn and spring, and fog is not uncommon.

The province of Brescia experiences on average 67.20 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 11.42 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 101.76 days per year. There are 20.40 foggy days throughout the year. Niardo receives around 7.3 hours of sunshine per day on average.
