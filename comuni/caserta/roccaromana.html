Roccaromana is a municipality of 841 inhabitants located in the Caserta province in the Italian region of Campania in Southern Italy. It has a population density of 30 people per km² and an altitude of 180 metres above the sea level.

Roccaromana accounts for about 0.09% of the total population in the province of Caserta and about 0.00140% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Roccaromana is classified as a Climate Zone D, which means it is a temperate town by Italian standards. The local climate is characterized by short, mild winters with hot and somewhat windy summers and overall little precipitation.

The province of Caserta experiences on average 107.11 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 0.51 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 101.40 days per year. There are 9.91 foggy days throughout the year. Roccaromana receives around 7.9 hours of sunshine per day on average.
