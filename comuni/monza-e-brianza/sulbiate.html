Sulbiate is a municipality of 4.394 inhabitants located in the Monza e Brianza province in the Italian region of Lombardy in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 845 people per km² and an altitude of 227 metres above the sea level.

Sulbiate accounts for about 0.50% of the total population in the province of Monza e Brianza and about 0.00729% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.
