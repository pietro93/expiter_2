Gambettola is a municipality of 10.661 inhabitants located in the Forlì-Cesena province in the Italian region of Emilia-Romagna in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 1.372 people per km² and an altitude of 31 metres above the sea level.

Gambettola accounts for about 2.70% of the total population in the province of Forlì-Cesena and about 0.01769% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Gambettola is classified as a Climate Zone E, which means it is a fairly chill town. The local climate is characterized by winters which are long and cold and summers which are dry and very hot. There are frequent precipitations in autumn and spring, and fog is not uncommon.

The province of Forlì-Cesena experiences on average 69.91 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 10.90 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 83.28 days per year. There are 26.50 foggy days throughout the year. Gambettola receives around 7.8 hours of sunshine per day on average.
