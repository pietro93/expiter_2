Senis is a municipality of 430 inhabitants located in the Oristano province in the Italian region of Sardinia in Southern Italy. It has a population density of 27 people per km² and an altitude of 256 metres above the sea level.

Senis accounts for about 0.27% of the total population in the province of Oristano and about 0.00071% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Senis is classified as a Climate Zone C, which means it is a fairly warm location. The local climate is characterized by short, mild winters and long, hot and dry summers. Rainfall is mostly concentrated to the winter period.

The province of Oristano experiences on average 90.51 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 0.00 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 77.76 days per year. There are 7.41 foggy days throughout the year. Senis receives around 8.2 hours of sunshine per day on average.
