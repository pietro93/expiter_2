Villalfonsina is a municipality of 908 inhabitants located in the Chieti province in the Italian region of Abruzzo in Southern Italy. It has a population density of 99 people per km² and an altitude of 203 metres above the sea level.

Villalfonsina accounts for about 0.24% of the total population in the province of Chieti and about 0.00151% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Villalfonsina is classified as a Climate Zone D, which means it is a temperate town by Italian standards. The local climate is characterized by cold winters and hot but windy summers, with somewhat frequent rainfall spread throughout the year.

The province of Chieti experiences on average 62.50 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 5.42 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 80.16 days per year. There is little to no fog throughout the year. Villalfonsina receives around 8.1 hours of sunshine per day on average.
