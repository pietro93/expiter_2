Roncà is a municipality of 3.740 inhabitants located in the Verona province in the Italian region of Veneto in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 206 people per km² and an altitude of 78 metres above the sea level.

Roncà accounts for about 0.40% of the total population in the province of Verona and about 0.00621% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.
