Aprilia is a municipality of 73.429 inhabitants located in the Latina province in the Italian region of Lazio in Central Italy. It has a population density of 412 people per km² and an altitude of 80 metres above the sea level.

Aprilia accounts for about 12.76% of the total population in the province of Latina and about 0.12185% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Aprilia is classified as a Climate Zone C, which means it is a fairly warm location. The local climate is characterized by short, mild winters with hot and somewhat windy summers and overall little precipitation.

The province of Latina experiences on average 97.99 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 0.00 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 89.04 days per year. There is little to no fog throughout the year. Aprilia receives around 8.6 hours of sunshine per day on average.
