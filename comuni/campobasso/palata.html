Palata is a municipality of 1.563 inhabitants located in the Campobasso province in the Italian region of Molise in Southern Italy. It has a population density of 36 people per km² and an altitude of 520 metres above the sea level.

Palata accounts for about 0.71% of the total population in the province of Campobasso and about 0.00259% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Palata is classified as a Climate Zone D, which means it is a temperate town by Italian standards. The local climate is characterized by long and cold winters with frequent snowfall in mountaineous areas, and somewhat warm and dry summers.

The province of Campobasso experiences on average 44.19 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 22.39 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 86.28 days per year. There is little to no fog throughout the year. Palata receives around 7.4 hours of sunshine per day on average.
