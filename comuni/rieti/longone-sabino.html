Longone Sabino is a municipality of 543 inhabitants located in the Rieti province in the Italian region of Lazio in Central Italy. It has a population density of 16 people per km² and an altitude of 804 metres above the sea level.

Longone Sabino accounts for about 0.35% of the total population in the province of Rieti and about 0.00090% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Longone Sabino is classified as a Climate Zone E, which means it is a fairly chill town. The local climate is characterized by short, mild winters with hot and somewhat windy summers and overall little precipitation.

The province of Rieti experiences on average 80.50 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 2.81 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 101.04 days per year. There are 12.00 foggy days throughout the year. Longone Sabino receives around 7.9 hours of sunshine per day on average.
