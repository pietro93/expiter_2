Baceno is a municipality of 867 inhabitants located in the Verbano-Cusio-Ossola province in the Italian region of Piedmont in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 11 people per km² and an altitude of 655 metres above the sea level.

Baceno accounts for about 0.55% of the total population in the province of Verbano-Cusio-Ossola and about 0.00144% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.
