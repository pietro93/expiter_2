Carmignano is a municipality of 15.098 inhabitants located in the Prato province in the Italian region of Tuscany in Central Italy. It has a population density of 393 people per km² and an altitude of 189 metres above the sea level.

Carmignano accounts for about 5.86% of the total population in the province of Prato and about 0.02505% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Carmignano is classified as a Climate Zone D, which means it is a temperate town by Italian standards. The local climate is characterized by short, mild winters with hot and somewhat windy summers and overall little precipitation.

The province of Prato experiences on average 76.39 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 4.49 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 86.16 days per year. There are 16.11 foggy days throughout the year. Carmignano receives around 7.6 hours of sunshine per day on average.
