Pontelongo is a municipality of 3.625 inhabitants located in the Padua province in the Italian region of Veneto in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 333 people per km² and an altitude of 5 metres above the sea level.

Pontelongo accounts for about 0.39% of the total population in the province of Padua and about 0.00602% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.
