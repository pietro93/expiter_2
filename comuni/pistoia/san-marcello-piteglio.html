San Marcello Piteglio is a municipality of 7.709 inhabitants located in the Pistoia province in the Italian region of Tuscany in Central Italy. It has a population density of 57 people per km² and an altitude of 623 metres above the sea level.

San Marcello Piteglio accounts for about 2.64% of the total population in the province of Pistoia and about 0.01279% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

San Marcello Piteglio is classified as a Climate Zone E, which means it is a fairly chill town. The local climate is characterized by short, mild winters with hot and somewhat windy summers and overall little precipitation.

The province of Pistoia experiences on average 71.31 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 5.21 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 88.56 days per year. There are 15.60 foggy days throughout the year. San Marcello Piteglio receives around 7.6 hours of sunshine per day on average.
