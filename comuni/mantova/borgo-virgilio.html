Borgo Virgilio is a municipality of 14.676 inhabitants located in the Mantua province in the Italian region of Lombardy in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 210 people per km² and an altitude of 22 metres above the sea level.

Borgo Virgilio accounts for about 3.56% of the total population in the province of Mantua and about 0.02435% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.
