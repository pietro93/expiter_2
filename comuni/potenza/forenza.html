Forenza is a municipality of 1.881 inhabitants located in the Potenza province in the Italian region of Basilicata in Southern Italy. It has a population density of 16 people per km² and an altitude of 836 metres above the sea level.

Forenza accounts for about 0.52% of the total population in the province of Potenza and about 0.00312% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Forenza is classified as a Climate Zone E, which means it is a fairly chill town. The local climate is characterized by long and cold winters with frequent snowfall in mountaineous areas, and somewhat warm and dry summers.

The province of Potenza experiences on average 35.49 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 17.01 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 81.60 days per year. There is little to no fog throughout the year. Forenza receives around 7.3 hours of sunshine per day on average.
