Faenza is a municipality of 58.335 inhabitants located in the Ravenna province in the Italian region of Emilia-Romagna in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 270 people per km² and an altitude of 35 metres above the sea level.

Faenza accounts for about 14.98% of the total population in the province of Ravenna and about 0.09680% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.
