Clivio is a municipality of 1.928 inhabitants located in the Varese province in the Italian region of Lombardy in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 646 people per km² and an altitude of 468 metres above the sea level.

Clivio accounts for about 0.22% of the total population in the province of Varese and about 0.00320% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Clivio is classified as a Climate Zone E, which means it is a fairly chill town. The local climate is characterized by winters which are long and cold and summers which are dry and very hot. There are frequent precipitations in autumn and spring, and fog is not uncommon.

The province of Varese experiences on average 39.70 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 16.59 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 104.76 days per year. There is little to no fog throughout the year. Clivio receives around 7.3 hours of sunshine per day on average.
