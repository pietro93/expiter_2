PORDENONE is a municipality of 51.818 inhabitants located in the Pordenone province in the Italian region of Friuli-Venezia Giulia in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 1.356 people per km² and an altitude of 24 metres above the sea level.

PORDENONE accounts for about 16.58% of the total population in the province of Pordenone and about 0.08599% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

PORDENONE is classified as a Climate Zone E, which means it is a fairly chill town. The local climate is characterized by cold winters and hot but windy summers, with somewhat frequent rainfall spread throughout the year.

The province of Pordenone experiences on average 71.31 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 6.31 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 104.64 days per year. There are 11.31 foggy days throughout the year. PORDENONE receives around 7.5 hours of sunshine per day on average.
