Savogna d'Isonzo is a municipality of 1.655 inhabitants located in the Gorizia province in the Italian region of Friuli-Venezia Giulia in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 97 people per km² and an altitude of 49 metres above the sea level.

Savogna d'Isonzo accounts for about 1.19% of the total population in the province of Gorizia and about 0.00275% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Savogna d'Isonzo is classified as a Climate Zone E, which means it is a fairly chill town. The local climate is characterized by cold winters and hot but windy summers, with somewhat frequent rainfall spread throughout the year.

The province of Gorizia experiences on average 69.50 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 7.61 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 100.92 days per year. There are 7.82 foggy days throughout the year. Savogna d'Isonzo receives around 7.6 hours of sunshine per day on average.
