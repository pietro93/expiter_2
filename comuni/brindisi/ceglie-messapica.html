Ceglie Messapica is a municipality of 18.991 inhabitants located in the Brindisi province in the Italian region of Apulia in Southern Italy. It has a population density of 144 people per km² and an altitude of 298 metres above the sea level.

Ceglie Messapica accounts for about 4.83% of the total population in the province of Brindisi and about 0.03151% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Ceglie Messapica is classified as a Climate Zone D, which means it is a temperate town by Italian standards. The local climate is characterized by cold winters and hot but windy summers, with somewhat frequent rainfall spread throughout the year.

The province of Brindisi experiences on average 69.91 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 0.62 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 76.68 days per year. There are 10.29 foggy days throughout the year. Ceglie Messapica receives around 8.5 hours of sunshine per day on average.
