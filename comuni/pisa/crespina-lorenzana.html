Crespina Lorenzana is a municipality of 5.393 inhabitants located in the Pisa province in the Italian region of Tuscany in Central Italy. It has a population density of 116 people per km² and an altitude of 86 metres above the sea level.

Crespina Lorenzana accounts for about 1.29% of the total population in the province of Pisa and about 0.00895% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Crespina Lorenzana is classified as a Climate Zone D, which means it is a temperate town by Italian standards. The local climate is characterized by short, mild winters with hot and somewhat windy summers and overall little precipitation.

The province of Pisa experiences on average 73.30 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 1.51 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 85.56 days per year. There are 8.81 foggy days throughout the year. Crespina Lorenzana receives around 8.1 hours of sunshine per day on average.
