Rapolano Terme is a municipality of 5.174 inhabitants located in the Siena province in the Italian region of Tuscany in Central Italy. It has a population density of 62 people per km² and an altitude of 334 metres above the sea level.

Rapolano Terme accounts for about 1.94% of the total population in the province of Siena and about 0.00859% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Rapolano Terme is classified as a Climate Zone D, which means it is a temperate town by Italian standards. The local climate is characterized by short, mild winters with hot and somewhat windy summers and overall little precipitation.

The province of Siena experiences on average 54.31 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 5.21 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 84.36 days per year. There are 7.30 foggy days throughout the year. Rapolano Terme receives around 8 hours of sunshine per day on average.
