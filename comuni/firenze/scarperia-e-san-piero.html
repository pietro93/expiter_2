Scarperia e San Piero is a municipality of 12.071 inhabitants located in the Florence province in the Italian region of Tuscany in Central Italy. It has a population density of 104 people per km² and an altitude of 292 metres above the sea level.

Scarperia e San Piero accounts for about 1.19% of the total population in the province of Florence and about 0.02003% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Scarperia e San Piero is classified as a Climate Zone E, which means it is a fairly chill town. The local climate is characterized by short, mild winters with hot and somewhat windy summers and overall little precipitation.

The province of Florence experiences on average 85.41 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 2.81 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 85.56 days per year. There are 16.01 foggy days throughout the year. Scarperia e San Piero receives around 7.7 hours of sunshine per day on average.
