Figline e Incisa V. is a municipality of 23.316 inhabitants located in the Florence province in the Italian region of Tuscany in Central Italy. It has a population density of 238 and an altitude of 122.

Figline e Incisa V. accounts for about 2.30% of the total population in the province of Florence and about 0.039% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.
