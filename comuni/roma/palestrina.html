Palestrina is a municipality of 21.949 inhabitants located in the Rome province in the Italian region of Lazio in Central Italy. It has a population density of 467 people per km² and an altitude of 450 metres above the sea level.

Palestrina accounts for about 0.51% of the total population in the province of Rome and about 0.03642% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Palestrina is classified as a Climate Zone E, which means it is a fairly chill town. The local climate is characterized by short, mild winters with hot and somewhat windy summers and overall little precipitation.

The province of Rome experiences on average 84.21 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 0.89 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 83.88 days per year. There are 6.51 foggy days throughout the year. Palestrina receives around 8.6 hours of sunshine per day on average.
