Fornovo di Taro is a municipality of 5.899 inhabitants located in the Parma province in the Italian region of Emilia-Romagna in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 103 people per km² and an altitude of 158 metres above the sea level.

Fornovo di Taro accounts for about 1.31% of the total population in the province of Parma and about 0.00979% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Fornovo di Taro is classified as a Climate Zone E, which means it is a fairly chill town. The local climate is characterized by winters which are long and cold and summers which are dry and very hot. There are frequent precipitations in autumn and spring, and fog is not uncommon.

The province of Parma experiences on average 84.51 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 20.91 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 83.76 days per year. There are 36.21 foggy days throughout the year. Fornovo di Taro receives around 7.7 hours of sunshine per day on average.
