San Benedetto in Perillis is a municipality of 97 inhabitants located in the L'Aquila province in the Italian region of Abruzzo in Southern Italy. It has a population density of 5,08 people per km² and an altitude of 878 metres above the sea level.

San Benedetto in Perillis accounts for about 0.03% of the total population in the province of L'Aquila and about 0.00016% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

San Benedetto in Perillis is classified as a Climate Zone E, which means it is a fairly chill town. The local climate is characterized by cold winters and hot but windy summers, with somewhat frequent rainfall spread throughout the year.

The province of L'Aquila experiences on average 56.40 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 16.22 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 75.96 days per year. There are 6.41 foggy days throughout the year. San Benedetto in Perillis receives around 7.6 hours of sunshine per day on average.
