Albettone is a municipality of 1.970 inhabitants located in the Vicenza province in the Italian region of Veneto in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 97 people per km² and an altitude of 19 metres above the sea level.

Albettone accounts for about 0.23% of the total population in the province of Vicenza and about 0.00327% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Albettone is classified as a Climate Zone E, which means it is a fairly chill town. The local climate is characterized by winters which are long and cold and summers which are dry and very hot. There are frequent precipitations in autumn and spring, and fog is not uncommon.

The province of Vicenza experiences on average 71.79 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 10.49 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 93.96 days per year. There are 22.22 foggy days throughout the year. Albettone receives around 7.6 hours of sunshine per day on average.
