Fiorano Modenese is a municipality of 16.981 inhabitants located in the Modena province in the Italian region of Emilia-Romagna in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 647 people per km² and an altitude of 115 metres above the sea level.

Fiorano Modenese accounts for about 2.41% of the total population in the province of Modena and about 0.02818% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Fiorano Modenese is classified as a Climate Zone E, which means it is a fairly chill town. The local climate is characterized by winters which are long and cold and summers which are dry and very hot. There are frequent precipitations in autumn and spring, and fog is not uncommon.

The province of Modena experiences on average 83.31 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 14.91 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 82.20 days per year. There are 29.31 foggy days throughout the year. Fiorano Modenese receives around 7.7 hours of sunshine per day on average.
