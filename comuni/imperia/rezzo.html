Rezzo is a municipality of 308 inhabitants located in the Imperia province in the Italian region of Liguria in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 8,24 people per km² and an altitude of 563 metres above the sea level.

Rezzo accounts for about 0.14% of the total population in the province of Imperia and about 0.00051% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.
