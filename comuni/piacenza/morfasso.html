Morfasso is a municipality of 926 inhabitants located in the Piacenza province in the Italian region of Emilia-Romagna in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 11 people per km² and an altitude of 631 metres above the sea level.

Morfasso accounts for about 0.32% of the total population in the province of Piacenza and about 0.00154% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Morfasso is classified as a Climate Zone F, which means it is one of the coldest locations in Italy. The local climate is characterized by winters which are long and cold and summers which are dry and very hot. There are frequent precipitations in autumn and spring, and fog is not uncommon.

The province of Piacenza experiences on average 78.62 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 24.51 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 81.84 days per year. There are 39.09 foggy days throughout the year. Morfasso receives around 7.6 hours of sunshine per day on average.
