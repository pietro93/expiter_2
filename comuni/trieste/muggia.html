Muggia is a municipality of 12.925 inhabitants located in the Trieste province in the Italian region of Friuli-Venezia Giulia in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 933 people per km² and an altitude of 3 metres above the sea level.

Muggia accounts for about 5.51% of the total population in the province of Trieste and about 0.02145% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Muggia is classified as a Climate Zone E, which means it is a fairly chill town. The local climate is characterized by cold winters and hot but windy summers, with somewhat frequent rainfall spread throughout the year.

The province of Trieste experiences on average 41.11 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 5.01 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 93.12 days per year. There are 8.91 foggy days throughout the year. Muggia receives around 7.8 hours of sunshine per day on average.
