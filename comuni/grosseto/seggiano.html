Seggiano is a municipality of 995 inhabitants located in the Grosseto province in the Italian region of Tuscany in Central Italy. It has a population density of 20 people per km² and an altitude of 491 metres above the sea level.

Seggiano accounts for about 0.45% of the total population in the province of Grosseto and about 0.00165% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Seggiano is classified as a Climate Zone E, which means it is a fairly chill town. The local climate is characterized by short, mild winters with hot and somewhat windy summers and overall little precipitation.

The province of Grosseto experiences on average 98.30 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 1.20 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 73.56 days per year. There is little to no fog throughout the year. Seggiano receives around 8.7 hours of sunshine per day on average.
