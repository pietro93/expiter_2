Quattro Castella is a municipality of 13.115 inhabitants located in the Reggio Emilia province in the Italian region of Emilia-Romagna in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 283 people per km² and an altitude of 161 metres above the sea level.

Quattro Castella accounts for about 2.47% of the total population in the province of Reggio Emilia and about 0.02176% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Quattro Castella is classified as a Climate Zone E, which means it is a fairly chill town. The local climate is characterized by winters which are long and cold and summers which are dry and very hot. There are frequent precipitations in autumn and spring, and fog is not uncommon.

The province of Reggio Emilia experiences on average 81.70 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 17.21 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 84.12 days per year. There are 32.19 foggy days throughout the year. Quattro Castella receives around 7.7 hours of sunshine per day on average.
