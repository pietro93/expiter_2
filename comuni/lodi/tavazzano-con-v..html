Tavazzano con V. is a municipality of 5.893 inhabitants located in the Lodi province in the Italian region of Lombardy in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 367 and an altitude of 82.

Tavazzano con V. accounts for about 2.56% of the total population in the province of Lodi and about 0.010% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.
