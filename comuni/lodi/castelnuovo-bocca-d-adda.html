Castelnuovo Bocca d'Adda is a municipality of 1.538 inhabitants located in the Lodi province in the Italian region of Lombardy in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 76 people per km² and an altitude of 49 metres above the sea level.

Castelnuovo Bocca d'Adda accounts for about 0.67% of the total population in the province of Lodi and about 0.00255% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Castelnuovo Bocca d'Adda is classified as a Climate Zone E, which means it is a fairly chill town. The local climate is characterized by winters which are long and cold and summers which are dry and very hot. There are frequent precipitations in autumn and spring, and fog is not uncommon.

The province of Lodi experiences on average 71.59 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 21.29 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 86.28 days per year. There are 49.61 foggy days throughout the year. Castelnuovo Bocca d'Adda receives around 7.5 hours of sunshine per day on average.
