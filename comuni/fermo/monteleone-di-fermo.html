Monteleone di Fermo is a municipality of 357 inhabitants located in the Fermo province in the Italian region of Marche in Central Italy. It has a population density of 43 people per km² and an altitude of 427 metres above the sea level.

Monteleone di Fermo accounts for about 0.21% of the total population in the province of Fermo and about 0.00059% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.
