Assisi is a municipality of 27.862 inhabitants located in the Perugia province in the Italian region of Umbria in Central Italy. It has a population density of 149 people per km² and an altitude of 424 metres above the sea level.

Assisi accounts for about 4.24% of the total population in the province of Perugia and about 0.04624% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Assisi is classified as a Climate Zone E, which means it is a fairly chill town. The local climate is characterized by long and cold winters with frequent snowfall in mountaineous areas, and somewhat warm and dry summers.

The province of Perugia experiences on average 42.89 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 10.49 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 84.84 days per year. There are 5.90 foggy days throughout the year. Assisi receives around 8.1 hours of sunshine per day on average.
