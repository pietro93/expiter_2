Collobiano is a municipality of 86 inhabitants located in the Vercelli province in the Italian region of Piedmont in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 9,32 people per km² and an altitude of 143 metres above the sea level.

Collobiano accounts for about 0.05% of the total population in the province of Vercelli and about 0.00014% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.
