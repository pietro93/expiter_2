Cisano sul Neva is a municipality of 2.089 inhabitants located in the Savona province in the Italian region of Liguria in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 170 people per km² and an altitude of 52 metres above the sea level.

Cisano sul Neva accounts for about 0.76% of the total population in the province of Savona and about 0.00347% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Cisano sul Neva is classified as a Climate Zone C, which means it is a fairly warm location. The local climate is characterized by short, mild winters with hot and somewhat windy summers and overall little precipitation.

The province of Savona experiences on average 46.49 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 2.50 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 75.48 days per year. There is little to no fog throughout the year. Cisano sul Neva receives around 7.6 hours of sunshine per day on average.
