Frattamaggiore is a municipality of 28.828 inhabitants located in the Napoli province in the Italian region of Campania in Southern Italy. It has a population density of 5.350 people per km² and an altitude of 44 metres above the sea level.

Frattamaggiore accounts for about 0.94% of the total population in the province of Napoli and about 0.04784% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Frattamaggiore is classified as a Climate Zone C, which means it is a fairly warm location. The local climate is characterized by short, mild winters with hot and somewhat windy summers and overall little precipitation.

The province of Napoli experiences on average 78.41 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 0.41 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 94.56 days per year. There is little to no fog throughout the year. Frattamaggiore receives around 8.3 hours of sunshine per day on average.
