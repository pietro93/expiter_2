Ronago is a municipality of 1.646 inhabitants located in the Como province in the Italian region of Lombardy in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 789 people per km² and an altitude of 357 metres above the sea level.

Ronago accounts for about 0.27% of the total population in the province of Como and about 0.00273% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Ronago is classified as a Climate Zone , which means it is . The local climate is characterized by winters which are long and cold and summers which are dry and very hot. There are frequent precipitations in autumn and spring, and fog is not uncommon.

The province of Como experiences on average 59.90 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 10.49 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 108.96 days per year. There is little to no fog throughout the year. Ronago receives around 7.2 hours of sunshine per day on average.
