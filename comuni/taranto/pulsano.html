Pulsano is a municipality of 11.201 inhabitants located in the Taranto province in the Italian region of Apulia in Southern Italy. It has a population density of 613 people per km² and an altitude of 37 metres above the sea level.

Pulsano accounts for about 1.94% of the total population in the province of Taranto and about 0.01859% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Pulsano is classified as a Climate Zone C, which means it is a fairly warm location. The local climate is characterized by cold winters and hot but windy summers, with somewhat frequent rainfall spread throughout the year.

The province of Taranto experiences on average 92.50 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 0.62 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 74.52 days per year. There are 10.59 foggy days throughout the year. Pulsano receives around 8.4 hours of sunshine per day on average.
