Ameglia is a municipality of 4.291 inhabitants located in the La Spezia province in the Italian region of Liguria in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 303 people per km² and an altitude of 89 metres above the sea level.

Ameglia accounts for about 1.95% of the total population in the province of La Spezia and about 0.00712% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.
