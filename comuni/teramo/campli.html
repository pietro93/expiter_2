Campli is a municipality of 6.714 inhabitants located in the Teramo province in the Italian region of Abruzzo in Southern Italy. It has a population density of 91 people per km² and an altitude of 393 metres above the sea level.

Campli accounts for about 2.18% of the total population in the province of Teramo and about 0.01114% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Campli is classified as a Climate Zone D, which means it is a temperate town by Italian standards. The local climate is characterized by cold winters and hot but windy summers, with somewhat frequent rainfall spread throughout the year.

The province of Teramo experiences on average 82.29 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 4.49 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 82.08 days per year. There is little to no fog throughout the year. Campli receives around 7.9 hours of sunshine per day on average.
