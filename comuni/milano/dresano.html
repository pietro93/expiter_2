Dresano is a municipality of 3.032 inhabitants located in the Milan province in the Italian region of Lombardy in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 872 people per km² and an altitude of 92 metres above the sea level.

Dresano accounts for about 0.09% of the total population in the province of Milan and about 0.00503% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Dresano is classified as a Climate Zone E, which means it is a fairly chill town. The local climate is characterized by winters which are long and cold and summers which are dry and very hot. There are frequent precipitations in autumn and spring, and fog is not uncommon.

The province of Milan experiences on average 69.29 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 9.50 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 91.56 days per year. There are 40.59 foggy days throughout the year. Dresano receives around 7.4 hours of sunshine per day on average.
